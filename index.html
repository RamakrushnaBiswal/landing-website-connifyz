    Highlights

    For the interview process: Successfully completing the first month of the internship is a prerequisite for eligibility. The interview process will be conducted after the completion of the internship. If you pass the interview, you will be eligible for a performance-based stipend ranging from 10,000 to 15,000 rupees. The stipend amount will reflect your performance and contribution during the internship. To participate in the interview process, there is a registration cost of 249/-.
    For opting out of the interview process: If you choose not to participate in the interview process, you have the option to pay 149/-. Paying this fee will grant you an Internship Completion Letter. These letters will confirm the successful completion of your internship and provide a recommendation for future opportunities.
